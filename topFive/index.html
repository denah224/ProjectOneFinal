top minecraft mobs

The Creaking, a hostile mob that only moves when no players are looking directly at it. This mob deals 3 hearts of damage.

The Enderman, a hostile mob that attacks the player when looked at. This mob deals 7 hearts of damage.

The Warden, found in the Deep Dark and is one of the strongest mobs. This mob deals 16 - 45 hearts.

The Wither Skeleton, found in the Nether and gives the player "withering". This mob deals 5 HP of damage over 10 seconds.

The Phantom, spawns only when the play hasn't slept for 3+ days. The worst mob ever, please take this thing out mojang.
